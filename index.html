The Vyr Programming Language
Paper-driven design process
Open standards effort

Designing Vyr through a written-first, AI-led standards process.

Vyr is a new programming language being developed in the open through a structured paper process. Each design decision is proposed, debated, and archived as a formal paper, enabling rapid exploration early and disciplined convergence over time.

The Vyr Foundation stewards the process and publishes all mailings, meeting notes, and consolidated specifications on this site.

How it works

Papers are released in mailings, discussed in meetings, and then recorded with decisions and follow-up actions. This keeps the language development transparent, traceable, and accountable.
